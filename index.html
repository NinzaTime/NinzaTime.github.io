Technical Writing Portfolio

In the cutthroat field of Cybersecurity, the line between safety and disaster can come down to one small detail: how risk is communicated. The works in this portfolio mark my journey from technical writer to rhetorician, connecting data and action through persuasion. In unpacking the specter of Post-Quantum Cryptography and dealing with the thorny questions of ethics in the profession, this portfolio shows an understanding of audience adaptation, turning intricate, logical arguments into stories that influence decision-makers. Whether you are a computer scientist looking for details or an executive weighing financial concerns, these artifacts provide a model for how rhetoric can be used to protect our digital destiny. Dive into this portfolio to discover how the precision of language can be just as important as the precision of programming when it comes to handling cybersecurity threats.
